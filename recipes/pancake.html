Pancake

Description

Fluffy, light pancakes that make a perfect breakfast or snack. You can serve them with honey, syrup, or fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix butter, garlic, and salt in a small bowl.
  2. Spread the mixture evenly on the bread slices

  5. Serve hot.
