Garlic Bread

Description

Crispy and buttery garlic bread that smells amazing and pairs perfectly with soups or pasta. Itâ€™s simple and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • A pinch of salt
  • Chopped parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix butter, garlic, and salt in a small bowl.
  2. Spread the mixture evenly on the bread slices.
  3. Toast the bread in a pan or oven until golden brown.
  4. Sprinkle parsley if desired.
  5. Serve hot.
